The Problem

Watching pro Dota 2 matches after the fact is frustrating. Tournament streams run for 6 to 8 hours covering multiple games. Finding the specific match you want means scrubbing through hours of content, guessing timestamps, and often landing in the wrong game entirely.

There was no easy way to jump directly to a specific match — until now.

What Spectate Esports Does

Timestamped VODs

Direct Twitch links that jump to the exact moment a match begins. No scrubbing.

Draft Breakdown

Full hero picks and bans with pro player names and KDA stats for every game.

AI Match Summaries

Draft analysis, strategy breakdown, MVP, and match highlights — generated by Claude.

Series Grouping

Games are grouped into BO1, BO3, and BO5 series with series scores.

Spoiler-Free Mode

Hide scores and match results until you're ready to reveal them. Toggle on/off anytime.

Shareable Match Cards

Every match has a unique link with a rich preview card. Share a specific game directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a match VOD?

Search for a team name or tournament on the homepage. Click any match to open the details panel and get a direct Twitch link timestamped to that exact game.

How far back do VODs go?

Twitch VODs are available for up to 60 days. Matches older than that will show no VOD found.

Why are multiple stream links shown?

When tournaments run concurrent matches on different sub-channels, all matching streams are shown. This is a known limitation — Twitch does not expose which stream carried which match.

What is the AI match summary?

Each match has an AI-generated summary covering draft analysis with a draft winner, team strategy, MVP, and a match highlight. Generated using Claude by Anthropic.

Is Spectate Esports free?

Yes. Spectate Esports is completely free with no account required.

Which data sources are used?

Match data is sourced from the OpenDota API. VOD links are sourced from the Twitch API. AI summaries are generated by Anthropic Claude.
